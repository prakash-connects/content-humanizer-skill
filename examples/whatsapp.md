# Example: WhatsApp Reply Humanization

## Input (AI-generated, High Risk)

> "I wanted to let you know that the meeting has been rescheduled to 3 PM tomorrow. It is important to note that all team members should be present. Furthermore, please ensure that you have reviewed the agenda beforehand to maximize productivity."

---

## Output (Humanized, Low Risk)

> "Hey — meeting's moved to 3 PM tomorrow. Everyone needs to be there, so block it out. And if you can glance through the agenda before joining, that'd help keep things moving."

---

## Key Changes Made
- Removed "It is important to note" and "Furthermore" (Tier 1 bans)
- Shortened from formal corporate tone to warm, direct WhatsApp style
- Added natural casual opener ("Hey —")
- Contractions throughout: "meeting's", "that'd"
- Punchy sentence rhythm: short → medium → short

## AI Risk: High → Low
